A little about me

Hello, my name is Lewis. I'm a designer & front-end developer based in the beautiful city of Bath, UK. I create websites for small companies and individuals all over the world. Currently, I am taking on a limited amount of freelance work through February. During the day I can be found at blubolt - where I work full time as part of the team to integrate designs for clients such as Whittard, Isabella Oliver and The Science Museum, to name just a few.
